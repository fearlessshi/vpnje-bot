## available command ##
/ovpn_online
/ovpn_info <username>
/ovpn_add <username> <password> <days>
/ovpn_renew <username> <days>
/ovpn_block <username>
/ovpn_unblock <username>
/ovpn_del <username>
/ovpn_reset <username> <new password>
/ovpn_block_test

/wg_info <username>
/wg_add <username> <days>
/wg_renew <username> <days>

/v2_info <username>
/v2_add <username> <days>
/v2_renew <username> <days>
