## available command ##
/ovpn_online
/ovpn_info <username>
/ovpn_add <username> <password> <days>
/ovpn_renew <username> <days>
/ovpn_block <username>
/ovpn_unblock <username>
/ovpn_del <username>
/ovpn_reset <username> <new password>
/ovpn_block_test

## example ##
/ovpn_info test
/ovpn_add testje 291220 31
/ovpn_renew testje 31
/ovpn_block testje
/ovpn_unblock testje
/ovpn_del testje
/ovpn_reset testje newpass
